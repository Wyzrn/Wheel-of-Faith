<rarity_targets>
<!-- CONT-01: Race rarity distribution (total weight determines probability) -->
Rarity class targets (percentage of total weight across all races):
- Common:    ~35% — weight 30–40 per race; 8–10 common races expected
- Uncommon:  ~45% — weight 12–20 per race; many uncommon races
- Rare:      ~18% — weight 4–9 per race; 5–8 rare races
- Legendary:  ~2% — weight 1 per race; 2–4 legendary races

Example race weights: Human 35, Orc 20, Halfling 18 (common), Elf 15, Gnome 12 (uncommon),
Dragon 8, Vampire 6 (rare), Celestial 1, Void-Born 1 (legendary).

Total weight across all 35+ races should be approximately 400–600 (weightedRandom normalizes).

Ability spin counts by rarity: Common typically 1, Uncommon 1–2, Rare 2–3, Legendary 3–4.
weaknessProbabilityModifier: Legendary races use 0.4–0.6 (fewer/lighter weaknesses),
high-durability races 0.6–0.8, standard races 1.0, "cursed" races 1.5–2.0.
</rarity_targets>
